About Belajar Academy

Belajar Academy is a learning community born from the ups and downs of facing academic challenges. Founded by Naufal Hadi Rizqullah in 2024, this platform serves as a space for students who want to grow, learn from their mistakes, and stay motivated despite past failures.

Our Vision

Our vision is simple yet powerful: to make quality education accessible to everyone. We believe that every student, regardless of their background, deserves equal opportunities to learn.

Our Mission

We provide a supportive community and learning resources to help students prepare for various academic competitions, such as science olympiads and other contests. Our materials are focused, experience-based, and designed to create an encouraging environment where students can grow without the fear of failure.

Our History

Belajar Academy was born from failure. Our founder experienced setbacks twice in the National Science Olympiad selection process. But those failures became the turning point to build a better, more inclusive learning platform. Many training programs are expensive, making them inaccessible to some students.
